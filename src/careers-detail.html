Paid Internship, Operations

Newark, Remote

Posted 5 months ago

This is a great opportunity to be part of our high-energy, fast-growing company ABF Creative. In this role, you will work with a diverse client portfolio of Fortune 500 Companies, Media Agencies, and private companies in the podcast and media world. You will work closely with the Chief Operating Officer and senior management team to manage projects, with a keen focus on our organizational strategy and business operations that directly influence project management. You will obtain hands-on experience while coordinating with stakeholders and functional managers within the company. Overall, your particular skill set and efforts will assist in driving ABF Creatives continuing business success.


Required Qualifications:

2+ years experience in podcast project management, business, and/or production Excellent leadership, organizational, and communication skills Proficient in project management productivity tools (i.e Basecamp, Asana, Slack) High attention to detail and focus, in a fast-paced environment Self-starter who can work to meet deadlines effectively while working remotely


Responsibilities:

Assist in managing the production timelines for pre-launch, launch and post-launch for original & branded content Assist in executing company process and procedures for the development and production of content to all departments Participate in weekly development & production meetings and share feedback Active participant in the onboarding and intake process of new clients Shadow the COO for the execution of operational procedures


Benefits of working with us:

Attain real-world experience from a network perspective Networking and making relevant connections Work with an award-winning team, with a depth of industry knowledge that can serve as mentorship
